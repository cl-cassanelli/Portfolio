Ciao, io sono Giuseppe!

I am a programmer born in 2005 who in my free time gives vent to the imagination and tries to create applications in order to simplify the online experience. I do not consider myself an expert let alone incapable of programming, in 2019 I was given Arduino One and since that time I've taken things more seriously. In the past I have tried my hand at creating a site, tblog.altervista.org, as can be seen from the domain it was created with Altervista. That project unfortunately lasted less than expected given the my inactivity in publishing articles on a daily basis. Through the free time of quarantine I became passionate about creation of bots for Discord and, one of them was successful: CL Music.

Projects

CL Music

Due to the forced quarantine I started studying Discord bot programming in Node.js; initially I created small shortcut to automate servers to the maximum and increase security. On 07-05-2021 I released CL Music, my first and only successful bot, scheduled in 5 weeks. It counts 21 commands divided into: 16 musical and 7 supplementary, in addition, the supported music playback platforms are YouTube and Spotify.

GamingHub

GamingHub Security Bot was my first sale involving private bots for Discord servers. As the name implies, it is intended for the semi-automatic moderation of a gaming server, but with internal commands also dedicated to users, such as memes, music etc. The bot was developed in Node.js and completed in about 1 week; thank the guys again for entrusting me with this job and for trusting me.
